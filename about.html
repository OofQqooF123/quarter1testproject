THIS IS

VALORANT

Valorant is an action packed multiplayer first person shooter that brings together shooting, tactical cooperation and distinctive character skills. Having multiple maps to play from, players will never get bored. It also involves two teams of five players each with one team attacking while the other defends.

With its own built-in voice chat feature, Valorant players will be able to communicate with each other. Bringing a level of coordination and teamwork to the game that is essential for success. Clear and effective communication is key, whether you're planning a well-timed attack, sharing information about enemy positions, or coordinating abilities with your teammates.
